About Us

BUCS is the result of years of experience attending and hosting Model United Nations and crisis conferences. The Model United Nations club is one of the largest student organizations at Brown University. For many years, we have hosted BUSUN, our high school conference, which is attended by nearly 800 students from around the United States. Over 100 volunteers work to make this conference happen and have gained valuable expertise in the process. In addition, our traveling team attends many conferences every year — from Harvard to Berkeley, as well as in Canada, Germany, and Hong Kong, among others. As a result, we know exactly what it takes to have a top-notch Model UN conference, and we will work tirelessly to make sure BUCS provides the best possible experience on the collegiate conference circuit.

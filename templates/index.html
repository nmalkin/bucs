The conference at a glance

Who?

  • One hundred college students from across the United States and around the world

What?

  • Joint crises: you will have to respond to decisions made by other committees
  • Settings for all tastes: modern, historical, and futuristic
  • Small committees: no more than 20 people each

Where?

  • Brown University
    Providence, Rhode Island
  • Providence is served by T.F. Green International Airport and is also easily accessible from Boston's Logan International Airport.

When?

  • Thursday, February 28, 2013
    – Sunday, March 3, 2013

Dear delegates and faculty advisors,

It gives us great pleasure to welcome you all to Brown University Crisis Simulation 2013! Set on Brown University’s historic Providence, Rhode Island campus BUCS will be held from Thursday, February 28th to Sunday, March 3rd 2013.

BUCS has always been driven by a delegate-first philosophy and we once again strive to provide students with a unique and amazing Model UN experience. At the heart of this is our joint crisis committee structure that puts you in the drivers seat. Our dedicated crisis staff has worked tirelessly to create a number of exciting simulations from the past, present and future, some traditional and some more creative.

Together with other intellectual and passionate students from around the world, delegates will assume crucial roles within one of our carefully created interconnected committees and tasked with the kind of constant decision-making and reaction demanded by the fast-paced environment in which crises unfold. Delegates will engage in intense negotiation and debate that we hope will push the limits of cooperation, adaptability, and creativity.

Yet we know that the conference experience extends beyond the committee room. From the champagne reception to club night, delegates will have the opportunity to practice their diplomacy in the friendlier and less stressful environs of our amazing private social events, perhaps even learning fellow delegates’ real names!

With our dedication to delegate experience, we sincerely hope that you will join us this February for what promises to be a uniquely exciting and unforgettable Model UN experience. If you have any questions at all, please do not hesitate to contact us. On behalf of the entire conference staff, we look forward to personally welcoming you to Brown next spring for BUCS 2013!

Sincerely,

Kevin O'Brien and Samantha Powell
Co-Secretaries General
BUCS 2013

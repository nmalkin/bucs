Location

BUCS 2012 will be held on the picturesque campus of Brown University, an Ivy League institution located in Providence's College Hill neighborhood. Brown University is located 5 minutes away from the heart of Providence, a city known for its many eateries, vibrant nightlife, and historical sites. Staying in downtown Providence and attending committee sessions on the Brown campus, delegates will enjoy all the amenities that this New England city has to offer and have the opportunity to experience some of the interesting traditions that make Brown students the happiest in the nation.

Entertainment Options

Dinner and Pub Night

Come relax after a long day in committee at McFadden's Restaurant and Saloon on Friday from 8pm-10pm for the BUCS Pub Night! In addition to providing bar food — wings, quesadillas, cheesesticks, crab rangoons — BUCS will be hosting an exclusive Trivia Night. Prizes will include free drink tickets for Saturday's club night as well as the coveted Trivia: Best Delegation Gavel!

McFadden's, one of Providence's most popular bars, is located at 52 Pine St. Providence, RI.

Club Night

Dance the night away with that special someone you met in committee at BUCS's Club Night! Come to Bravo Brasserie's Lounge from 10pm-1:30am on Saturday night to hear Brown's own DJ Postal spinning a progressive mix of top 40, house, techno, dubstep, hip-hop, popular music and everything in between. DJ Postal has been co-signed by everyone from Diddy to Rihanna and continues to escalate his credentials.

Bravo, a hotspot for Brown students, is located at 123 Empire St. Providence, RI.

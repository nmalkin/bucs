Conference Logistics

BUCS 2012 will be held on the picturesque campus of Brown University, an Ivy League institution located in Providence’s College Hill neighborhood. Brown University is located 5 minutes away from the heart of Providence, a city known for its many eateries, vibrant nightlife, and historical sites. Staying in downtown Providence and attending committee sessions on the Brown campus, delegates will enjoy all the amenities that this New England city has to offer and have the opportunity to experience some of the interesting traditions that make Brown students the happiest in the nation.
